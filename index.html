Privacy Policy for NewsPulse

Last updated: 9 JUL 26

This Privacy Policy explains how NewsPulse ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By downloading, installing, or using NewsPulse, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

We built NewsPulse to bring you breaking headlines, live scores, market updates, and weather in one place, and we take the privacy of everyone who uses it — in the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, India, and elsewhere — seriously.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information collected automatically

When you use NewsPulse, certain information is collected automatically to operate the app, serve advertisements, and improve performance:

  • Device information: device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
  • Advertising identifiers: Google Advertising ID (Android), or equivalent identifiers, used to serve and measure ads
  • Usage data: articles viewed, features used, session length, and in-app interactions
  • Approximate location: derived from IP address or device settings, used to show relevant local news and weather (only if you grant location permission)
  • Log and diagnostic data: crash reports, app performance data, and error logs

1.2 Information you provide directly

  • Preferences you set within the app (e.g., topics you follow, notification settings)
  • Any information you submit if you contact us for support

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App
  • To personalize your news feed and content recommendations
  • To serve and measure the performance of advertisements
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or abuse
  • To analyze usage trends and improve app performance
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Advertising and analytics partners listed in Section 2, to serve ads and measure app performance
  • Service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., cloud hosting, crash reporting)
  • Legal authorities, where required by law, regulation, or valid legal process
  • A successor entity, in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Children's Privacy

NewsPulse is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 For users in the European Economic Area and UK (GDPR)

If applicable to you, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data, and the right to data portability. You can exercise these rights by contacting us using the details below.

6.2 For California residents (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion of personal information, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. NewsPulse does not sell personal information for monetary value; ad personalization through our partners may be considered "sharing" under CPRA, and you can opt out via your device's ad settings or by contacting us.

6.3 For users in India

In accordance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, we process personal data only for the purposes described in this policy and provide you the ability to withdraw consent and request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6.4 For users in Canada and Australia

We handle personal information in accordance with applicable privacy laws, including Canada's PIPEDA and Australia's Privacy Act 1988. You may contact us to access or request correction of your personal information.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, where our service providers operate. We take steps to ensure your data is treated securely in accordance with this policy wherever it is processed.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
